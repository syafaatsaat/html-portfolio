The Best Movies According to Syaf

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Mr Bean's Holiday

This is my favourite comedy film. I've always been a fan of Mr Bean.

Ratatouille

I love the setting and the storyline. Childhood favourite.

Charlie and the Chocolate Factory

It has a nice storyline. The songs in the movie are catchy and makes you want to dance. I always watch it when I am on a flight.
